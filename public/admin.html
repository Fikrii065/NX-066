Cara Kerja Markup

Harga jual = Modal + max(Modal × %, Minimum)

Contoh: Modal Rp 50.000, markup 5%, min Rp 500
→ Profit = max(2.500, 500) = Rp 2.500
→ Jual = Rp 52.500
