📖 Cara Kerja Markup

Harga jual = Modal + max(Modal × %, Minimum Rp)

Contoh — Modal Rp 50.000, Markup 5%, Min Rp 500
→ 5% × 50.000 = Rp 2.500 (≥ Rp 500 ✓)
→ Harga jual = Rp 52.500
Contoh — Modal Rp 3.000, Markup 5%, Min Rp 500
→ 5% × 3.000 = Rp 150 (< Rp 500, pakai minimum)
→ Harga jual = Rp 3.500
